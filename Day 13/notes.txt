Difference bw console.log() & document.write

We use console object methods to show output on the browser console and we use document.write to show output on the browser document(view port).

However, Both methods are used only for testing and debugging purposes.
The console method is the most popular testing and debugging tool on the browser.

In addition to the famous, console.log() method, the console provides other more methods.

console obj methods:
    console.log()
    console.warn()
    console.error()
    console.table()
    console.time()
    console.info()
    console.assert()
    console.group()
    console.count()
    console.clear()
